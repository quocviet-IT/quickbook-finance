## Wave Apps and QuickBooks Online findings

The results below record only information that can be verified from official sources. Items without clear confirmation are marked as “could not be determined.”

>
> The 2022 QuickBooks Australia manual documents the historical workflow of direct feed/file upload, For Review, Categorised, Excluded, matching by amount/date/payee, and reconciliation. It is supporting UX evidence, not confirmation of current bank support in Vietnam. [Q15]

## Primary-candidate findings

### 2.1 Oracle NetSuite

| **Perspective / question group**                                   | **Verified finding**                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
|--------------------------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **A1–A4: Accounting, manufacturing, and international operations** | NetSuite is a cloud ERP combining accounting, order management, inventory, and enterprise processes. OneWorld supports subsidiaries with separate base currencies and consolidated reporting using consolidated exchange rates. The platform has the strongest multi-entity and multi-country capability in this shortlist. Manufacturing capabilities include BOM, routing, work-order, inventory, and supply-chain functions, although jewelry-specific costing requires configuration or customization. [S4][S5]                                                                                                 |
| **P1–P4: Workflow and user experience**                            | Sales, inventory, fulfillment, accounting, and CRM can be managed on a common platform. Dashboards are role-oriented, and OneWorld supports subsidiary-level reporting. Vietnamese implementation and localization are typically delivered by a local implementation partner rather than through a purely self-service product setup. [S5][S8]                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| **R1–R4: Vietnam compliance and retention**                        | NetSuite provides Vietnam VAT Return functionality, including reports supporting Form 01/GTGT and related schedules. Direct VAT submission was not verified. Vietnam e-invoicing is implemented through localization or connector partners such as BTM and local providers including SInvoice or MISA meInvoice, rather than a native direct-tax-authority connection. Current Decree 254/2026 readiness was not verified and must be contractually confirmed. NetSuite performs frequent cloud backups, but legal retention, export, immutability, and document presentation must be separately designed. [S6][S7] |
| **D1–D4: Data residency**                                          | NetSuite is normally delivered as Oracle cloud SaaS. Public materials reviewed do not identify a Vietnam-specific data center for an individual NetSuite tenant, and standard deployment is not on-premise. CTYHP should assume potential overseas processing and require a DPA, data-flow map, subprocessor list, retention terms, and cross-border transfer assessment. [S8]                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| **I1–I4: Integration architecture**                                | SuiteTalk REST Web Services, OAuth 2.0, and English technical documentation are available. A TypeScript server-side adapter can integrate NetSuite with Supabase mapping, outbox, webhook, and audit tables. Integration is API-oriented rather than limited to Excel files, but enterprise implementation often requires partner-led configuration and customization. [S9]                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| **B1–B4: Pricing and commercial model**                            | No public VND price list was identified. NetSuite is quotation-based and normally combines SaaS subscription, users, OneWorld, manufacturing modules, localization, migration, implementation, integration, and support. Five-year TCO must be obtained through an RFP. [S8]                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |

*Interim assessment: NetSuite is the strongest enterprise and multi-country option, but approval requires written confirmation of Decree 254 readiness, Vietnam tax workflows, data-center location, cross-border processing terms, and total implementation cost.*

### 2.2 Odoo

| **Perspective / question group**                                   | **Verified finding**                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
|--------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **A1–A4: Accounting, manufacturing, and international operations** | Odoo integrates Accounting, Sales, Purchase, Inventory, Manufacturing, Quality, and PLM. It supports multi-company, multi-currency, and consolidated reporting. Its shared modular data model can support an end-to-end order → BOM → manufacturing → inventory → invoice flow. Jewelry-specific requirements such as karat/purity, fine-gold equivalent, stone allocation, scrap recovery, assay, and process-loss rules were not verified as native capabilities and will probably require custom modules. [S10]                                                                 |
| **P1–P4: Workflow and user experience**                            | Because sales, inventory, manufacturing, and accounting operate within one suite, Odoo can provide a more continuous operational workflow than an accounting-only platform. English user and developer documentation is extensive, and country localization packages are available. [S10][S11]                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| **R1–R4: Vietnam compliance and retention**                        | Odoo has an official Vietnam fiscal localization and a Viettel SInvoice integration. Invoices created in Odoo can be synchronized to SInvoice for issuance. Tax return reports support preparation of tax data; direct VAT filing was not verified. The reviewed documentation did not prove that the connector has been updated for Decree 254/2026, so the vendor and SInvoice provider must confirm the exact supported version and workflow. Cloud backup is documented, while self-hosting permits CTYHP to design its own legal archive and retention controls. [S11][S12] |
| **D1–D4: Data residency**                                          | Odoo supports Odoo Online, Odoo.sh, and self-hosted/on-premise deployment. Public cloud regions do not identify a dedicated Vietnam region, but self-hosting permits CTYHP to place the database on infrastructure located in Vietnam. This is the strongest data-residency flexibility among the global platforms in the shortlist. [S13]                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| **I1–I4: Integration architecture**                                | Odoo provides a full developer framework and external APIs with English documentation. Recent versions are moving toward JSON-2 while older RPC interfaces are being deprecated. Under a Custom plan, Odoo.sh, or self-hosted deployment, CTYHP can build modules and integrate directly with Next.js, TypeScript, Supabase, and Vercel. [S14]                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| **B1–B4: Pricing and commercial model**                            | The Vietnam pricing page publishes per-user subscription pricing in USD rather than VND. The Custom plan is required for API use, multi-company, Studio, custom modules, Odoo.sh, or on-premise deployment. Official implementation service packs are published, while partner customization and jewelry-specific modules are additional costs. [S15]                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |

*Interim assessment: Odoo currently offers the best balance of manufacturing coverage, integration flexibility, self-hosting, customization, and cost. The critical unresolved item is current Decree 254 e-invoice readiness and the acceptable VAT-filing path.*

### 2.3 MISA SME

| **Perspective / question group**                                   | **Verified finding**                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
|--------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **A1–A4: Accounting, manufacturing, and international operations** | MISA SME is strongly aligned with Vietnamese accounting. It supports export sales, foreign-currency transactions, exchange rates, exchange differences, inventory, and production-costing functions. Some company/branch consolidation is supported, but global multi-country consolidation equivalent to NetSuite OneWorld was not verified. [S16][S17] |
| **P1–P4: Workflow and user experience**                            | The product is designed for Vietnamese accountants and domestic tax-document processes. It supports accounting, inventory, and costing workflows, but no evidence was found that MISA SME is a complete manufacturing ERP with routing, work centers, PLM, and shop-floor execution. [S17]                                                                 |
| **R1–R4: Vietnam compliance and retention**                        | MISA has the clearest evidence of current Vietnam regulatory readiness in this comparison. MISA SME 2026 and meInvoice materials describe updates for the new e-invoice and tax framework, including Decree 254/2026. MISA mTax and related workflows support tax preparation and electronic submission or export to the relevant tax tools. [S18][S19]  |
| **D1–D4: Data residency**                                          | MISA SME can be deployed using SQL Server on company-controlled computers or servers, allowing the main accounting database to remain in Vietnam. Cloud services such as meInvoice, mTax, eSign, or support still create external data flows; MISA should therefore provide a DPA, data-center details, and subprocessor information. [S20]                |
| **I1–I4: Integration architecture**                                | A public, English-language API/SDK specifically for MISA SME was not identified. MISA publishes Open API material for some AMIS products, but this must not be assumed to apply to MISA SME desktop. Integration with Next.js/Supabase may depend on vendor APIs, database integration, import/export, or a vendor-developed connector. [S21]              |
| **B1–B4: Pricing and commercial model**                            | MISA publishes VND pricing and offers annual and one-time licensing options. Public pricing identified for the Standard tier was approximately VND 4.65 million per year or VND 8.85 million for a one-time license. meInvoice, mTax, digital signatures, invoice volumes, training, and customization may be charged separately. [S22]                    |

*Interim assessment: MISA SME is the strongest statutory-compliance candidate and has favorable local data residency and VND pricing. Its main limitations are public API maturity, English technical documentation, international consolidation, and full manufacturing-ERP coverage.*

### 2.4 FAST Accounting

| **Perspective / question group**                                   | **Verified finding**                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
|--------------------------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **A1–A4: Accounting, manufacturing, and international operations** | FAST Accounting includes cash, sales, purchasing, inventory, fixed assets, tools, projects, and production-costing functions. It supports export transactions, foreign-currency receivables, and exchange-rate differences. It is stronger than a basic bookkeeping package for production costing, but evidence of full global ERP functions such as PLM, advanced routing, shop-floor execution, and multi-country consolidation was not identified. [S23][S24] |
| **P1–P4: Workflow and user experience**                            | FAST is suitable for Vietnamese businesses requiring production accounting and management reports. English product material exists, but most operational help and technical material is Vietnamese. Advanced international user support and cross-country governance require verification. [S24]                                                                                                                                                                    |
| **R1–R4: Vietnam compliance and retention**                        | FAST products integrate with Fast e-Invoice. The vendor has published updates regarding the new e-invoice and tax framework, including Decree 254 and related 2026 guidance. However, a product release note proving every required operational workflow was not identified; CTYHP should require a live demo and contractual commitment. Tax reports are supported, but direct VAT submission was not verified. [S25][S26]                                       |
| **D1–D4: Data residency**                                          | The desktop/server edition can be installed on a company computer or LAN server, enabling local data storage in Vietnam. For online products, the exact data-center location was not verified. If residency is mandatory, CTYHP should select an on-premise deployment or require the hosting location in the contract. [S27]                                                                                                                                       |
| **I1–I4: Integration architecture**                                | FAST has delivered API, middleware, banking, and e-commerce integrations, showing that project-based integration is possible. A public developer portal with complete API schemas, sandbox, OAuth, and English SDK documentation for FAST Accounting was not identified. Integration with Next.js/Supabase is therefore likely to be vendor-led. [S28]                                                                                                              |
| **B1–B4: Pricing and commercial model**                            | FAST publishes VND pricing. The Manufacturing package was listed at approximately VND 11.9 million for packaged software, plus around VND 4.45 million for initial training and consulting. Customization and API integration are quoted separately. [S26]                                                                                                                                                                                                          |

*Interim assessment: FAST is a credible local candidate for production costing, foreign-currency accounting, on-premise data, and transparent VND pricing. It requires deeper verification of API access, multi-entity capability, direct VAT filing, and current Decree 254 product workflows.*

## QuickBooks Online — outstanding research questions

The following table closes four evidence gaps in the QuickBooks Online baseline. Where official sources do not provide a definitive answer, the finding is stated as not confirmed rather than inferred.

| **Question**                                                              | **Finding**                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       | **Source**                                                                                                                                            | **Confidence level**                   |
|---------------------------------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------|
| **1. Data residency and Decree 13/2023/ND-CP**                            | Intuit confirms that QuickBooks Online files are stored in the cloud using Amazon Web Services infrastructure, but the Global FAQ does not identify the specific AWS region used for a Vietnam-based account. Intuit also reserves the right to store and process personal information in the United States and other countries where it or its service providers operate. No reviewed Intuit document states that QBO is certified or contractually compliant with Vietnam's Decree 13/2023/ND-CP. The exact tenant region and Decree 13 compliance are therefore not confirmed; deployment would require written confirmation of data location, subprocessors, cross-border transfer safeguards, retention terms, and the applicable data-processing agreement. | [Q16] QuickBooks Global FAQ; [Q17] Intuit Global Privacy Statement; [Q18] Vietnam Decree 13/2023/ND-CP.                                         | Official doc                           |
| **2. Estimated implementation TCO for 20–50 accounting/operations staff** | A defensible typical implementation cost cannot be determined from published evidence without assumptions. Published partner prices describe smaller engagements: basic setup/training at USD 395, advanced consulting at USD 750, integrated consulting at USD 1,500, and training for a 2–5-user team at USD 999.95. A Fourlane migration case study documents custom scoping, data correction, migration, and dedicated support but does not publish the project price. QBO Advanced supports up to 25 users, so a 20–50-person organization requires access-role planning and may exceed the published direct-user limit. A custom implementation quote is required.                                                                                          | [Q19] Hector Garcia consulting prices; [Q20] QuickBooks Training team pricing; [Q21] Fourlane migration case study; [Q1] QBO plan comparison. | Marketing page / partner documentation |
| **3. Accounting API rate limits**                                         | The production Accounting API limits are 500 requests per minute per realm ID, 10 concurrent requests in one second per realm ID and app, and 40 batch requests per minute per realm ID, with a maximum of 30 payloads in a batch. The current official document does not publish a daily request quota. Intuit recommends webhooks and Change Data Capture for synchronization and requires clients to handle HTTP 429 throttling. The limits are technically compatible with many near-real-time invoice/payment synchronization patterns, but sufficiency for CTYHP is not confirmed until transaction volumes, burst behavior, retry logic, and load tests are measured.                                                                                      | [Q22] Intuit Developer Support — API call limits and throttling.                                                                                    | Official doc                           |
| **4. USD billing and foreign-exchange risk**                              | The QuickBooks Global storefront displays subscription prices in US dollars and accepts major credit/debit cards. Monthly and annual upfront billing are available. The reviewed official pages do not identify an option to invoice or settle the Global subscription in VND, nor a mechanism to lock a fixed USD/VND exchange rate. Annual prepayment can fix the USD subscription charge for the prepaid term under the applicable offer, but it does not fix the VND conversion rate; the final VND cost depends on the card issuer's exchange rate and any foreign-transaction fees.                                                                                                                                                                         | [Q23] QuickBooks Global account plan comparison; [Q16] QuickBooks Global FAQ.                                                                     | Official marketing page                |
